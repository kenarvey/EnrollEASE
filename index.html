Enrollment Procedure

STEP 1

Link: College Applicant's Portal

Log in to College Admission Applicant's Portal:(https://dione.batstate-u.edu.ph/batstateucat/portal/#/). Proceed to "Student Portal" after pre-registration. Remember your Student Portal Log In credentials.

STEP 2

Afterwards, proceed to the Online Appointment System, click "Select Events". Input your SR Code (00-00000) for Log In.

STEP 3

Select an available schedule once all enrollment requirements are COMPLETE.

STEP 4

Confirm the selected schedule. An email confirmation can also be checked.

STEP 5

Click "View" to see your appointment/ schedule. A printed appointment slip shall be presented to the guard on duty.

STEP 6

You may click the "Cancel" button if you intend to cancel your scheduled appointment.

STEP 7

Submit the COMPLETE enrollment requirements to the Registrar's Office ON THE APPOINTMENT DATE you have selected on the online appointment system. The Registrar/Registration Staff will ONLY tag those qualifiers as ENROLLED who complied with the submission of enrollment documents.

STEP 8

AFTER ENROLLMENT: Login to your Student Portal at https://dione.batstate-u.edu.ph/student/#/. Click “ID Capturing” button and follow the instructions/steps indicated therein.

STEP 9

Click “View Subject Status” to check if you have enrolled the complete courses as stated in your curriculum for First Year, First Semester.
